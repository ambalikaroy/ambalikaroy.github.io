Impact of Land Use on GDP

Does Land Use or Abuse really impact a country's GDP?


Objective


In this project we will look at the various land usage patterns across the world and the impact it has on the GDP. We examine if there is any correlation between certain types of land usage and the GDP.


Design


I will be using the various components of Narrative visualization, Scenes, Annotations, Paramters and Triggers in this project.

This narrative visualization follows the structure of an interactive slideshow allowing the user to traverse the slides and look at different factors and their impact or non-impact on GDP.


Data


I am using the World Development Indicators dataset from World Bank. This dataset originally is available as an Excel file from World Bank and is free to use.

The original dataset contained over 1500 development indicators measured across 264 countries over a period of 60 years from 1960 to 2020.

However, for the purpose of this project, I considered only a subset of indicators related specifically to land usage and for a shorter time period: a decade from 2005 through 2015.
