Does land usage impact GDP?

What kind of land usage might impact a country's GDP?

More fertile land and higher rural population definitely have a positive effect on the GDP.

Major portion of the land in most of the countries in the world is occupied by the rural population

But developed and developing countries have a higher proportion of urban land area. That alone cannot conclusively impact GDP.
